Tortilla scrambled eggs

Mouthwatering and simple recipe? Serves 1 (serves 1...)

Scavange your fridge for a healthy, fast breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tortilla shredded or stripped cut
  • 3 tablespoon of (choose 2 or dont)
    • chopped peppers
    • Chopped onion
    • chopped tomatoes
    • chopped potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons shredded cheese (your choise of cheese)
  • a pinch of salt or black pepper (optional)

Directions

  1. heat butter in skillet over medium. cook tortilla shreds/strips.
  2. add your veggies(experimenting is encourage) cook 2-3 minutes.
  3. add eggs, scrable, cook throughly and add cheese and sessoning last.
