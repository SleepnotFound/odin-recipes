Guacamole

A delicious few ingredients recipe. Serves 4 (Usually)

No heat, no problem. grab ya knife,bowl and cutting board and lets get chopping

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of
    • chopped cilantro
    • Chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 jalapeno pepper seeded and minced(optional spicyness)
  • salt and ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Peel and pit avocados. mash 'em until creamy! (in a bowl of course)
  2. Mix tomatoes,cilantro,onion,lemon juice, and jalapeno.
  3. Season to taste and enjoy
