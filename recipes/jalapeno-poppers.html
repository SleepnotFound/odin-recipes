Jalapeno poppers

A quick minimal ingredients recipe. Serves 6 (or 1,your choice)

Savor these easy spicy poppers made with 4 ingredients! no excuses, go prep this right now actually

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 12 japapeno peppers halved lenghtwise and gutted
  • 12 slices bacon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F(200 degrees C).Line baking sheet with aluminum.
  2. mix cream cheese and cheddar evenly. Stuff cheese goodness into each jalapeno halve and sandwitch. Wrap each with bacon and settle in backing sheet,
  3. Bake until crispy bacon(15 minutes or so)
  4. Profit.
